About Dhansruti Group

Dhansruti Group stands as a testament to visionary leadership and unwavering commitment to excellence in the construction and real estate industry. Since our founding in 2014, we have grown from a small team with big dreams into a powerhouse of over 500 professionals dedicated to transforming the built environment across India.

Headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, we have established ourselves as one of the region's most trusted names in construction, delivering landmark projects that blend innovation, quality, and sustainability. Our portfolio spans residential complexes, commercial developments, and infrastructure projects that shape communities and drive economic growth.

With a diverse expertise across construction, business services, and textile manufacturing, we bring a multifaceted approach to every project. Our growth trajectory reflects our commitment to excellence, innovation, and creating lasting value for our clients, partners, and stakeholders.
